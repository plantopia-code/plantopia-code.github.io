Flashing Heart

Learn how to use the LEDs and make a flashing heart!
(Want to learn how lights work? Watch this video).





Step 1

Place the show leds block in the forever block and draw a heart.





Step 2

Place another show leds block. You can leave it blank and draw what you want.





Step 3

Look at the virtual micro:bit, you should see the heart and your drawing blink on the screen.





Step 4

If you have a micro:bit connected, click     Download to transfer your code and watch the hearts flash!
