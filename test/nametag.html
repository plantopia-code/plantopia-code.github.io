Name Tag

Tell everyone who you are. Show you name on the LEDs.





Step 1

Place the show string block in the forever block to repeat it. Change the text to your name.





Step 2

Look at the simulator and make sure it shows your name on the screen.





Step 3

Place more show string blocks to create your own story.





Step 4

If you have a micro:bit connected, click     Download tto transfer your code and watch your name scroll!
